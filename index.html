Frequently Asked Questions

How does the pheromone technology work?

Our pads are infused with a specially designed pheromone that mimics the natural scent signals dogs use to mark their territory. This scent is undetectable to humans but irresistible to dogs, naturally attracting them to use the pad as their designated bathroom spot without any training needed.

Is the PuppyPad really chew-proof?

Yes! The PuppyPad is constructed with military-grade, tear-resistant fabric. We've tested it against the most determined chewers. While no material is 100% indestructible, our pads are designed to withstand typical chewing, shredding, and digging, unlike flimsy disposable pads.

How do I wash the PuppyPad?

It's simple! Just toss it in the washing machine on a regular cycle with your usual detergent. You can then tumble dry on low or hang it to dry. The pad is designed to withstand over 300 washes, maintaining its absorbency and odor-control properties.

Will my house smell like dog pee?

Absolutely not. The PuppyPad features a 4-layer system with an antimicrobial core that traps and eliminates odors on contact. The super-absorbent material locks away liquid instantly, so your home stays fresh and clean between washes.

How many PuppyPads do I actually need?

We highly recommend our "Buy 3, Get 3 Free" bundle. This allows you to have a seamless rotation: one pad in use, one in the wash, and a clean one ready. For larger homes or multiple dogs, this ensures you always have a fresh spot available, which is key to preventing accidents.
